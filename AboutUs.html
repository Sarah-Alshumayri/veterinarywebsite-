WELCOME TO SOFT TOUCH CLINIC

Soft touch clinic offers wellness, management and health related pet care services for your cats, dogs and other companion animals. We have a comfortable, collaborative approach to your pet's care. This means, the pet care services are provided with you involved in each step along the way. We provide vast types of service from traditional treatments and care options such as routine vaccinations to complicated surgery and dentistry by well experienced doctors on the field.

ABOUT US

Soft touch clinic was found in 2006 to give you the finest pet care service. We are a competitive team of professionals who specializes in providing pet care service for small and companion animals including health services with quality, individualized attention to our clients and their pets in an atmosphere of respect, warmth and honesty. The entire staff at this hospital is experienced, helpful and friendly.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is "Pet Care from the Heart". We strive continually to provide the best health care and preventive veterinary care services for your pet while keeping in mind the needs and desires of their owners. To provide high quality products and services to pet owners and any interested parties from both governmental and private sectors in the Kingdom. In Support of these activities, we perform meaningful clinical practices and services for the improvement of pet health. The entire staff at this hospital is experienced, helpful and friendly.

OUR VISION

To be the first choice for pet owners and interested parties in the Kingdom.
